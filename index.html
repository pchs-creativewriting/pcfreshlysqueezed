Creative Writing Club

The PCHS Creative Writing Club is open to all students wanting to participate. Here, we aim to increase students' abilities to write, create ideas, and lead.

Throughout the course of the year, Creative Writing Club members will create literary works, either for just themselves, or for Freshly Squeezed, the annual PCHS literary journal. Members will also vote on school-wide submissions for the journal.

If you:

  • are an avid author
  • are a creator of worlds
  • wish to improve your writing skills

it is highly recomeneded that you join the creative writing club. Learn more here or email us for more information.

Submit:

The Creative Writing Club accepts literary pieces as well as art pieces to be placed in Freshly Squeezed. We encourage all students with an affinity for writing or art (in any form) to submit their masterpieces by emailing them to pchsfreshlysqueezed@gmail.com.

Please format your submissions to the following guidelines:

  • Double spaced
  • 12pt Times New Roman font
  • Name centered under title
  • Please limit your submission to # pages

For art submissions, please scan and submit as a PNG. You can find a tutorial on this here and you can use the school digital scanner in room ###.

Join:

We're always looking for new members! If you have a love of writing, creating worlds, or wish to improve your writing skills, then the Creative Writing club is your club. We meet after school from 2:30-3:30 in room 142 of the highschool on Thursdays every week. Come drop by or contact us at pchsfreshlysqueezed@gmail.com for any further inquiries or to seek membership.

FAQ's (for Freshly Squeezed and Creative Writing Club):

  • When and where is the club?: 2:30-3:30 in room 142, only on Thursdays.
  • What does a typical club session look like?: While this is up to change, for the first 30 minutes we typically write seperately. We then join together to address club business such as marketing Freshly Squeezed, taking part in writing workshops, and goofing off.
  • If I'm in the club, does that automatically mean my work is guaranteed to be in Freshly Squeezed?: No, but it does give you a better chance.
  • What types of submissions do you accept?: We accept art, poems, comedies, short stories (please limit to # pages) play and movie manuscripts, and many more. Contact us to see if your piece qualifies.
